Geodesic Fréchet Mean vs Euclidean Mean

Fréchet mean: θ* = argmin_θ ∑ w_i · d_FR(θ, θ_i)² — the point on the manifold minimising weighted geodesic distances.

Curvature-Based Heterogeneity

κ-heterogeneity = mean geodesic distance from Fréchet mean / expected distance under homogeneity. Unlike I², this captures non-Euclidean spread on the manifold.
